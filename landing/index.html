FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the AI receptionist work?
When a customer calls your number, Twilio routes the call to our AI. The AI greets the caller, understands their intent using advanced language models, and handles the conversation naturally — booking appointments, answering questions, or taking messages.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
Yes! You can port your existing business number to Twilio, or we can provide you with a new number. The setup takes just a few minutes.
What happens in an emergency?
The AI is trained to detect emergencies and urgent situations. It will immediately transfer the call to your emergency number or a human team member. You can set custom emergency rules in settings.
Can I customize how the AI sounds?
Absolutely. You can choose from multiple AI voices, set the tone (formal, friendly, or casual), and customize the greeting message. Enterprise plans offer custom voice cloning.
How does billing work?
We use a simple subscription + usage model. Your monthly plan includes a set number of call minutes. If you exceed your limit, extra minutes are billed automatically at $0.08/min. No surprises.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with 50 call minutes. No credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.
